Help & Support

Welcome to the BlockvoteX Help Center. We are here to assist you with any questions or issues you may have while using our platform. Below, you’ll find helpful guides, FAQs, and resources to ensure you have a smooth and secure experience.

Getting Started

New to BlockvoteX? Here's how to begin:

  1. Voter Registration: Visit our Registration Page to create your account. You’ll need to provide your email, full name, government-approved voter ID, picture, and address.
  2. Admin Verification: After registration, your account will be reviewed by the admin team. You’ll receive your login credentials via email once verified.
  3. Logging In: Use the email and admin-provided password to access your account and participate in elections.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q: How do I register to vote?
    A: You can register by visiting the Voter Registration Page and filling in your details. After admin verification, you’ll receive your credentials.
  • Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
    A: Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the Login Page, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions to reset your password.
  • Q: How can I view election results?
    A: Once logged in, go to the "Election Results" section on your dashboard to view the latest results and previous election data.
  • Q: Can I update my profile information?
    A: Yes, after logging in, navigate to your profile page and make necessary changes. Admins may need to verify any major changes.
